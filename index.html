*Expansion and Growth:*

- *2001s-2005s:* The studio quickly gained a reputation for innovative design solutions. During this period, Rekha studio expanded its services and opened additional offices in DMM (2006), GTP(2007). Each office operated independently while maintaining the Rekha studio ethos.

*Key Projects and Contributions:*

1. *2000s:*

Rekha studio work during this period included iconic projects such as the redesign of the *ATP City subway map* and the visual identity for *The Museum*.

2. *20001s:*

The studio continued to evolve, undertaking significant projects such as the *Mastercard logo redesign* in 2003 and branding work for *United Airlines*.

3. *2002s-Present:*

Rekha studio has remained influential in the design world with notable projects including the redesign of the *Windows 8 interface* and the visual identity for the *London 2012 Olympics*.

*Design Philosophy and Approach:*

Rekha studio is known for its diverse and client-focused approach, with each partner bringing their unique style and expertise. Their work often emphasizes simplicity, clarity, and creativity, blending traditional graphic design principles with contemporary needs.

*Legacy and Influence:*

Rekha studio is renowned for its impact on graphic design, setting standards for quality and innovation. The studio's multidisciplinary approach and its ability to adapt to changing design landscapes have cemented its reputation as one of the most respected design firms globally.
